## 1. Data controller

Juro Spain is the controller of personal data collected through this website and our service delivery channels. Contact us at [hello@jurospain.com](mailto:hello@jurospain.com).

## 2. Information we collect

* Identification details (name, passport numbers, NIE)
* Contact details (email, phone, address)
* Immigration and property information relevant to your case
* Usage data from our website (cookies, analytics if consented)

## 3. Why we process data

We process personal data to provide requested services, comply with legal obligations, manage billing, and send relevant updates. We rely on contractual necessity, legitimate interest, or consent depending on the processing activity.

## 4. Sharing data

We share data with trusted partners such as regulated lawyers, translators, notaries, and technology providers that support secure document handling. All partners follow EU data protection standards.

## 5. International transfers

When data leaves the EEA, we use safeguards like Standard Contractual Clauses or adequacy decisions.

## 6. Data retention

Personal data is retained for the duration of our engagement plus the period required by Spanish law (typically 5 years) unless a longer period is necessary to comply with regulations.

## 7. Your rights

You may request access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, or data portability. Email [hello@jurospain.com](mailto:hello@jurospain.com) to exercise these rights.

## 8. Cookies

We use essential cookies and optional analytics cookies. Analytics cookies only load after you accept them via our cookie banner.
