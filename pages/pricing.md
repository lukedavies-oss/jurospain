## Choose the right level of support

| Plan | Ideal for | Deliverables | Investment |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Starter | EU citizens needing NIEs, empadronamiento, or bank setup | 1:1 onboarding call, document prep, appointment scheduling, post-visit checklist | €490 |
| Relocator | Non-EU families applying for non-lucrative or Digital Nomad Visas | Visa strategy workshop, document review, consulate submission kit, arrival-day TIE support, insurance coordination | €1,650 |
| Investor | Property buyers or Golden Visa investors | Pre-purchase due diligence, power of attorney drafting, notary representation, tax coordination, residency filings for family members | €4,800 |

### How billing works

1. **Discovery call.** Free 20-minute consultation to map your timeline and confirm eligibility.
2. **Engagement letter.** Fixed scope with transparent add-ons if your plan evolves.
3. **Milestone payments.** 50% to kick off, 30% before submissions, 20% on completion.

Looking for ongoing support? We offer retainers starting at €190/month for compliance monitoring, annual tax reminders, and ad-hoc consultations.
