## Why Juro Spain exists

We launched Juro Spain after helping friends navigate a maze of NIE appointments, property closings, and visa renewals. The process was slow, confusing, and rarely available in English. Today we provide a single point of contact who coordinates translators, sworn lawyers, notaries, and tax advisors so you can focus on relocating.

## A bilingual, tech-enabled concierge

* Dedicated legal concierge fluent in English and Spanish
* Partner law firm registered with the Madrid Bar Association
* Transparent digital workspace with shared timelines and document checklists
* Nationwide coverage for Spain’s major cities and coastal hubs

## Meet our partner lawyer

Claudia Martínez leads our legal practice. With 12+ years advising international investors and families, she specialises in investor visas, property acquisitions, and complex residency renewals. Claudia and the team coordinate closely with our English-speaking client success specialists.

## Trusted by global professionals

* Remote workers applying for the Digital Nomad Visa
* Non-EU families securing Golden Visas and non-lucrative permits
* Buyers acquiring primary residences and holiday homes across Spain
* Entrepreneurs registering as autónomos and structuring their taxes

## Our values

1. **Clarity first.** We translate legal requirements into plain English.
2. **Momentum always.** We proactively book appointments, chase paperwork, and keep your move on track.
3. **Partnership mindset.** We collaborate with your tax planner, wealth manager, or employer to deliver a cohesive plan.
